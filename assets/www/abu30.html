OMUMU JISUS

"Nna-ayi ezitewo Okpara-Ya ka O buru Onye-nzoputa nke uwa"
1 Jon 4. 14.
Good Christian men rejoice.
  1. Ezi ndi Kraist. nuria
    N'olu na nkpur'obi!
    Ge nti n’ihe ayi nekwu;
    Ozi!
    Ta k'amuru Jisus Kraist,
    An'ulo da n'ukwu-Ya
    O no n'ulo-anu ugbu a;
    Ta k’amuru Kraist.
  2. Ezi ndi Kraist, nuria
    N'olu na nkpur'obi!
    Akuk’onu mgbe nile;
    Onu!
    Ya mere ka Kraist ji bia:
    O meghew'uzo igwé,
    Madu b’onye agoziri:
    Ya mor'O ji bia.
  3. Ezi ndi Kraist, nuria
    N’olu na nkpur'obi!
    Uj’onwu adigh ozo:
    Udo!
    Jisus Kraist biar’izota,
    Nakpo ayi n’otu n’otu
    Ir’ulo ebigh-ebi-Ya!
    O biar’izota. Amen.
