UTUTU

"Ole mgbe I gabiakute?"
Abu 0ma 101. 2.
Come to me, Lord, when first I wake.
  1. Biakutem, Kraist, mgbe m'tetara,
    Mgbé anw’ututu nawata;
    K’ezie’echiche batam n'isi
    Dik’igirigi si n’igwé.
  2. Biakutem n’oke ehihie,
    Ka nsogbu nile di n'uwa
    Ghar’ikpuch’ihé iru-Gi,
    We me k’ubochim gbachita.
  3. Biakutem na mgbe anyasi;
    Asi na obim kpafuru,
    Kpolata ya, ma mukwasim
    Iru-oma-I ka kpakpando.
  4. Biakutem nime abali
    Mgbe ura gefepu n’anyam;
    Ka nkpur’obim hu iz’ike,
    Ka Jon si hu, n’obi Jisus.
  5. Biakutem n’uzo ndu nile;
    Mgbe obim kubiri ume,
    Ka m'lakuru I, Onye-nzotam,
    Ka nwa-I noyere Gi n’igwé. Amen.
