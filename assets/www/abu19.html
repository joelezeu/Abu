OMUMU JISUS

"N'ihi na amuworo ayi otu nwa, otu nwa-nwoke ka enyeworo ayi."
Aisaia 9. 6.
Hark! the herald angels sing.

  1. Ge nti! ndi-m'ozi nabu
    Neto Ez’amur'ohu;
    Udo di n'e1u' uwa,
    Chuku na ndi-njo edo!
    Nuria onu mba nile,
    Soro n’onu nke igwé;
    Igwe na ala nasi,
    Ta k’amuru Jisus Kraist.
  2. Kraist, Onye nd'igwé Kraist.
    Kraist, Eze ebigh-ebi naja;
    Na mgbe nke-Ya k'O biara,
    Nwa nke Agbogho di nso.
    Le, Chuku yiri aru;
    To Onye ghoto madu;
    O toro Y'igho madu;
    Jisus Kraist, Immanuel.
  3. Kelenu Eze udo,
    Kel'Onye-ez'omume;
    Ihe na Ndu k’O ji bia,
    Nagwo oria madu dum.
    O rapur’ebube-Ya,
    Bia ka madu ghar'inwu,
    Ka madu nile bilie,
    Ka amukwa ha ohu.
  4. Bia, Onye mba dum nacho.
    Biri nime ayi nile;
    Bilie, Nkpuru nke nwayi,
    Chifia isi nke Agwo.
    Hichap'onyinyo Adam.
    Tiyechie onyinyo-Gi;
    Nye madu dum Onwe-Gi,
    Kpua Onwe-Gi n'obi-ayi.   Amen.
