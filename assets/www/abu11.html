CHINEKE NNA.

"Le, i werewo nehegbu a nile ehegbu onwe-gi bayere ayi"
2 Ndi Eze 4. 13
God, who made the north.

  1. Chuk'Ony'Okike
    Nke uwa n'el’igwe,
    Onye ker'anyanwu,
    Nelekotam.
  2. Onye ker'ohia,
    Osisi na nkpuru,
    Ehihie n'abali,
    Nelekotam.
  3. Onye ker'anwu,
    Qnwa na kpakpando,
    Mgb'ahuhu biara, O
    Gelekotam.
  4. Ya ziri Nwa-Ya
    K'O nwua na Kalvari;
    Ma m'dabere Ya, O
    Gelekotam.
  5. Mgbe m'ruru n'igwé,
    Ma hu ndi-eyi-Ya,
    M'geso ndi nag bua: O
    Lekotaram!   Amen.
