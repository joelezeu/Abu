ANYASI

"Noyere ayi: n’ihi na o rubewo anyasi, ubochi agamiwo kwa ugbu a."
Luk 24. 29.
Abide with me: fast falls the eventide.
  1. Noyerem: o rubewo anyasi;
    Ochichi nagba: Jisus, noyerem;
    Mgb’iye-aka na nkasi gwusiri,
    Gi, Iye-aka nkem, bia noyerem.
  2. Ubochi ndu-ayi nagafe ngwa ngwa,
    Onu n’otuto nk’uwa nka nete;
    Ngbanwe n’uré di n’akuku nile:
    Gi, Onye nenwegh ngbanwe, noyerem.
  3. Abiala n’egwu, dik’Eze nk'igwe;
    Kama, bia n’obi’oma igw’oria dum;
    Gi, Onye-nkasi-obi n’ahuhu,
    Eyi nke ndi-nmehie, bia noyerem.
  4. O dim nkpa k’I noyerem mgbe nile,
    Nani amara-I ganyi Ekwensu;
    Onye dika Gi gabu Onye-ndum?
    N’udu-miri n’okochi, noyerem.
  5. M'I noyerem m'gagh-at’ujo nd'iro;
    Nsogbu n’anya-miri adigh ilu;
    Onwu, ole ebe nmeri-gi di?
    Anom nemeri ma Gi noyerem.
  6. Gosim obe-I mgbe m’gekpuchi anyam,
    Nenwu na ngbachi, zim uzo nk'igwe
    Ch'igwé nabo, onyinyo uw’aga:
    Na ndu, n’onwu, Onye-nwem, noyerem. Amen
