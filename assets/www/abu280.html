UMU-NTAKIRI.

"Ayil onwe-ayi amawo kwa ihu-n’anya anu, ayi ekwerewo kwa na ya, bu nke Chineke nwere n’ebe ayi no."
1 Jon 4. 16.
Praise Him! praise Him! all ye little children.
  1. To Ya, to Ye, un’umu-nta nile:
    O bu Ihu-n’anya;
    To Ya, to Ya, un’umu-nta nile:
    O bu Ihu-n’anya!
  2. Nekele Ya, un’umu-nta nile:
    O bu Ihu-n’anya;
    Nekele Ya, un'umu-nta nile:
    O bu Ihu-n'anya!
  3. Hu Ya n’anya, un’umu-nta nile:
    O bu Ihu-n’anya;
    Hu Ya n'anya, un’umu-nta nile:
    O bu Ihu-n’anya!
  4. Me Ya Eze, un umu-nta nile:
    O bu Ihu-n’anya;
    Me Ya Eze, un’umu-nta nile:
    O bu Ihu-n'anya! Amen.
