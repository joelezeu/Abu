UMU-NTAKIRI.

"N’udo ka m’geji otu mgbe ahu dina ala, raru urs: n’ihi na Gi onwe-gi, Jehova, nani Gi, neme ka m’biri na tukwasi-Obi."
Abu Oma 4. 8.
Jesus, tender Shepherd, hear me.
  1. Jisus, Onye-nchem, nur’olum,
    Gozie nwa-I n’abali ta;
    Nodu nsom nim'ochichiri,
    Chebem rue chi-obubo.
  2. Aka-I durum n’ubochi ta,
    Ekelem Gi n’ihi ya;
    I nyerem nri, n’uwe, n’ulo:
    Na nti ekper'anyasim.
  3. Gbaghara nmehie nkem nile,
    Gozie ndi m’huru n’anya;
    Mgbe m’nwuru kurum la n’igwé
    Ibiyere Gi n’onu. Amen.
