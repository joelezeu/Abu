OMUMU JISUS

"Ya mere, neohenu nche: n’ihi na unu amatagh ubochi Onye-nwe-unu gabia."
Matiu 24. 42.
When Jesus comes to reward His servants.
  1. Ma jisus bia ile ndi-Ya olu,
    N’ehihie m’ob’aba1i,
    O gahu ayi ezi ndi-Ya na nche,
    Jide oku-ayi nenwu?

    2. Koros:- Ayi gasi n’ayi ejikere, nwannem,
            Iba n’ulo nk'e1'igwe?
            O gahu mu na gi k’ayi neche nche,
            Neche, neche mgbe Ez’ayi gabia?

  2. Ma o buru na isi-ututu,
    Ka O gakpo mu na gi,
    Ka ayi gosi Ya olu ayi luru,
    O gasi, “I mere nk’oma?”
  3. A mezuru ihe O si ayi me,
    Neme otu ik'ayi ra?
    Ma obi-ayi adigh-am’ayi ikpe,
    Ayi gez’ike n’otuto.
  4. Ngozi diri ndi O gahu na nche,
    Ha geket'ebube-Ya:
    M'O bia n’ututu m’obu n’abali,
    O gahu k’ayi neche nche?   Amen.
