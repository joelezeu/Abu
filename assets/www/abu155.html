OTUTO

"Gi, Onye-nwe-ayi na Chineke-ayi, I kwesiri imeta otuto na nsopuru nile na ike nile."
Nkpughe 4. 11.
Praise Him! Praise Him! Jesus, our blessed Redeemer!
  1. To Jisus, Onye-ngbaputa nke di ngozi!
    K’uwa nabu ok’ihu-n’anya-Ya;
    Kele Ya, nd,isi-m’ozi nke bi n’otuto,
    K'ike na nsopuru dir’aha-Ya!
    Jisus geche umu-Ya dik’aturu,
    Kw’ubochi k’O neku ha n’aka-Ya.
    2. Koros:-To Ya! To Ya! nekwu na Ya bu
          Okaka.
    To Ya! To Ya! n’abu ohu mgbe
          dum.
  2. To Jisus, Onye-ngbaputa nke di ngozi
    O nwuru n’ihi mehie-ayi nile;
    Ya bu nkume nzoputa ebigh-ebi-ayi,
    Kele Jisus, Onye akpogburu!
    Nye Y’otuto, Onye hur’ayi ahuhu;
    Ihu-n’anya nke nenwegh ih'atu!
  3. To Jisus, Onye-ngbaputa nke di ngozi!
    Hosanna nakpotu nim’el’igwe.
    Jisus Kraist gabu eze mgbc ebigh-ebi
    Me Ya Eze, ndi-nzuko-Ya nile!
    O nabia dik’Onye-nmeri n'el’uwa,
    Ike' n’otuto diri Onye-nw’ayi! Amen.
