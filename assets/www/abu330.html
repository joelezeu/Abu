ARO OHU

"Mo nke Onye-nwe-ayi di n’arum . . . Ikwusa aro mgbe Onye-nwe-ayi nanara madu nke-oma"
Luk 4. 18-19.
Another year is dawning.
  1. Ar’ohu namalite;
    Onye-nw’ayi, k’o buru,
    N’olu m’obu n’iche-nche,
    Aro nke diri Gi.
  2. Aro ntukwasi-obi,
    Nk’ebere n’amara;
    Ar’ohu nk’ezi onu
    Nk’iru-I gamukwasi.
  3. Aro nke iga-n’iru,
    Aro nke otuto;
    Ar’ohu nke nnwaputa
    Nnoyere-Gi mgbe dum.
  4. Aro nke ije-ozi,
    N’ama ihu-n’anya-I;
    Ar’ohu nke njikere
    Ilu olu n’igwé.
  5. Ar’ohu namalite:
    Onye-nw'ayi, k’o buru,
    Nim’uwa m’obu n’igwé,
    Aro nke diri Gi. Amen.
  6. Nime onwunwa o bula,
    Chineke gechebe i;
    Ony'ume-ngwu, kwere na Ya!
    Chineke gechebe i! Amen.
