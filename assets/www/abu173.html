"Chineke, ka obi-Gi juru n'ebe mu, bu onye-nmehie no."
Luk 18. 13.
Sinful, sighing to be bleat
  1. Mu, di njo, nacho ngozi,
    Nacho ka m’puta n'oru,
    Nechere izu-ike:
    Chuku, merem ebere.
  2. Enweghm ezi agugo,
    Nmehie di n’olum nile,
    Nani nkpam ka m’nebute:
    Chuku, merem ebere.
  3. Obi-ntiwa n’ihere
    Ekweghm iweli anyam,
    Ma Gi mar’udé m’nasu:
    Chuku, merem ebere.
  4. Site n’obi-nmehie nka
    Ka m'gbakuru nani Gi;
    Abugh nkem, kama nke-Gi:
    Chuku, merem ebere.
  5. Onye no ns’och’eze-Gi
    Ka m'tukwasiri obim;
    Enweghm iye’aka ozo:
    Chuku, merem ebere.
  6. Ya ganekwurum onum,
    Ya gasughari okwum;
    Ya bu nkem, na n’ihi Ya,
    Chuku, merem ebere. Amen.
