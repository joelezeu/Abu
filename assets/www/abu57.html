OMUMU JISUS

"Ka eke unu ihe-okike n'ukwu,ka orioma-umu nenwu kwa"
Luk 12. 35.
Ye servants of the Lord.
  1. N di-ozi Onye-nw’ayi,
    Onye na olu-ya,
    Necheta okwu O kwuru
    Neche n’on’uzo-Ya.
  2. K'oku-unu nenwu,
    Dozienu ya nk’oma:
    Kenu ih’okike n’ukwu,
    Aha-Ya di egwu.
  3. "Chenu nche! "b'iwu Nn'ayi;
    O no kwa nso ugbu a;
    Le ih'ama obibia-Ya,
    Ka unu jikere.
  4. Ony'agahu na nche
    Genwu ezi onu!
    O geji onu hu Jisus,
    Agasopuru ya.
  5. Kraist geji aka-Ya
    We dozie oriri,
    Welite ony'ozi ahu
    N'iru ndi-m'ozi-Ya.   Amen.
