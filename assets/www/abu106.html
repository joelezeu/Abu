UBOCHI ONYE-NWE-AYI

"Ya mere ka ihere ama nke Onye-nwe-ayi ghara imo gi"
2 Timoti 1. 8.
In token that thou shalt not fear.
  1. K'i ghar’it’ujo ib’onye
    Nke Kraist akpogburu,
    Ayi aka obe-Ya n’iru-i,
    Me gi nani nke-Ya.
  2. K’ihere ghara ime gi
    Ito aha nso-Ya,
    Ayi me k’aput’ihé n’iru-i
    Nleda n’otuto-Ya.
  3. K’i ghar’ilaghachi azu
    N’iluru Kraist ogu,
    N'okpuru okoloto-Ya
    Jis’ike ka nwoke.
  4. K’i mara na i ghagh iso
    Uzo nk’O gafere,
    Buru obe, lel’ihere,
    Nodu ala n’igwé.
  5. Ayi si otu a n’iru ora
    Ka gi nke-Ya n’ezie;
    K’iru nke nebu obe-Ya
    Keta okp’eze-Ya! Amen.
