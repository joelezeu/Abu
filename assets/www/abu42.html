NBILITE-N'ONWU JISUS.

"Emere ka O si n’onwu bilie."
Mak 16. 6.
Christ the Lord is risen today,
  1. Kraist Ez'ayi biliri ta: Haleluya!
    Ka madu na m’ozi si Haleluya!
    Kwesienu abu ike; Haleluya!
    Igwe na ala, kwenu. Haleluya!
  2. O lusiwo olu-Ya; Haleluya!
    O luwo ogu, meme; Haleluya!
    Anwu nk'ezi-omume; Haleluya!
    O gagh-anwu kwa ozo. Haleluya!
  3. N di-nche neche nche n'efu; Haleluya!
    Kraist etiw’uzo ili; Haleluya!
    Qnwu ejidegh kwa Ya! Haleluya!
    Kraist emeghewo igwé. Haleluya!
  4. N di-nche neche nche n'efu; Haleluya!
    Kraist etiw’uzo ili; Haleluya!
    Qnwu ejidegh kwa Ya! Haleluya!
    Kraist emeghewo igwé. Haleluya!
  5. Eze-ayi di ndu ozo; Haleluya!
    Onwu, ole ike-gi? Haleluya!
    O nwuru, k’ayi we di ndu; Haleluya!
    Ili, ole nmeri-gi? Haleluya!
  6. K’ayi rigokuru Ez’ayi, Haleluya!
    Neso Ya, bu Isi-ayi; Haleluya!
    Ayi geyi Ya, we bilie; Haleluya!
    Nadi ndu nmeri nk’obe. Haleluya!
  7. To Eze igwé n’uwa, Haleluya!
    K’ihe nile neto Gi; Haleluya!
    Onye-nmeri, ayi neto I, Haleluya!
    To Gi, Nbilite-n’onwu. Haleluya!   Amen.
