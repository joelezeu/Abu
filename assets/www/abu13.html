CHINEKE OKPARA

"Aha ahu nke kariri aha nile o bula elu"
Ndi Filipai 2. 9.
How sweet the name of Jesus sounds.

  1. Aha Jisus di ok’uto
    Na nti Onye kwere,
    Q nakasi ya n’ahuhu,
    Nachupu egwu-ya.
  2. O nagwo Qria nkpur'obi,
    Najur’obi nsogbu;
    O bu nri nke Onye-agu,
    Nenye izu-ike.
  3. Nkume ebei m’wuru ulom,
    Qtam n’Ebe-ngbabam;
    Ulo-akum di mgbe nile,
    Juputa n'amara.
  4. Jisus, Eyim, Onye nazum,
    Ezem, Ony'ozizim,
    Onye-nwem, na Ndum na Uzom,
    Nar'otuto m'ji bia.
  5. Obim jiker'ike adigh,
    Echichem jur'oyi;
    Ma mgbe m’gahu Gi k'I si di,
    Aghaghm itbzui Gi.
  6. Ka m'kwusa ihu-n'anya-Gi.
    Site n’ikem nile;
    K'Aha-Gi nke tokar'uto
    Nye obim ndu n'onwu.   Amen.
