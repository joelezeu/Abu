NRIGO N'ELU-IGWE JISUS

"I ji kwa nsopuru na, ima-nina kpube Ya n'isi dika okpu-eze."
Abu Oma 8. 5.
The Head that once was crowned with throne.
  1. Isi nke kpururi ogwu
    Nekpu otuto ta;
    Okpu-eze di ebube
    Jisus Dinkpa kpuru.
  2. Oche nke kachasi elu
    Bu oké-Ya n’ezie;
    Eze nd’eze, Onye-nw'ora,
    Ihé ebigh-ebi.
  3. Onu ndi nile bi n'igwe,
    N’onu ndi bi n'uwa,
    Ndi mara ihu-n'anya-Ya,
    Ndi kwere n'aha-Ya.
  4. Ha kere oke n’ihere,
    Na ngozi nke obe;
    Aha-ha di mgb'ebigh-ebi.
    Onu-ha bu nk’igwé.
  5. Ha, nahu ahuhu n’uwa,
    Ha b’eze n'igwe;
    Imar’ihu-n’anya-Ya, bu
    Uru na onu-ha.
  6. Onwu ihere O nwuru
    Nenye ndi nke-Ya ndu;
    Obe-Ya bu olil'anya
    Na otuto nke-ha. Amen.
