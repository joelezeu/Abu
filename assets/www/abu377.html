ABU NKE NDI-IGBO CHEPUTARA AKWUKWO NSO.

"Kpughepu anyam abua, ka m'we legide Oke di ichie iche si n’iwu-Gi puta."
Abu Oma 119. 18.
  1. Nna, meghe obi-ayi
    Inu okwu nke-Gi;
    Ka site n’ime ya
    Ayi we bur-umu-Gi.
  2. Wepu adigh-ike
    Nke netufu okwu-I;
    Tiye Mo Nso nke-Gi
    Nim’obi-ayi nile.
  3. Me k’ayi debe okwu
    Nke ayi ganu ugbu a;
    Chup’ajo ony’ahu
    Nke narafu ndi-Gi.
  4. Zitere ayi Mo Nso,
    K'O nezi ayi kari
    Ime uche nke-Gi
    N’ikwere Ozi’oma. Amen.
