UBOCHI ONYE-NWE-AYI

"Obim nile ka m’wereworo eho Gi; Ekwela ka, m'si n’ihe mile I nyere n’iwu jehie."
Abu Oma 119. 19.
My God, accept my heart this day.
  1. Chukum, nara nkpur’obim ta,
    K'O buru nke-I mgbe dum;
    Ka m’ghar’irapu Gi kpafue,
    Ghar’idaghchi'azu.
  2. N'iru obe nk’Onye nwuru,
    Le, ka m’gbur’ikpere;
    Kwe k’akpogbue nmehie nile,
    Ka Kraist bur’ihe dum.
  3. Tem manu amara nk'igwé,
    Me kwa ka m’buru nke-I;
    Ka m'hu iru otuto-Gi,
    Fe Gi n’och'eze-Gi.
  4. K’echiche, n’o1u n’okwu dum
    Buru nke-Gi mgbe dum;
    Jisus, ka m’were ndum fe Gi,
    Onwu gab'uz'igwe!   Amen.
