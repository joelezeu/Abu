"Nani idi-uma na ebere gagbasom ubochi nile nke ndum."
Abu Oma 23. 6.
For Thy mercy and Thy grace.
  1. N'ih ebere-I-n'amara-I
    N’aro gafere ugbu a,
    Nuru abu ekele,
    Jisus, Onye-ngbaput'ayi.
  2. Ayi amagh odi-n’iru;
    Dur’ayi, Kpakpand’ututu;
    Agha na nd’iro d’ike:
    Jisus, lu-kwa-r’ayi ogu.
  3. N'ahuhu n’adigh-ike
    Buru Nkume d'ike-ayi;
    Nime nfughari nk’uwa
    Bur'ezi Uzo ndu-ayi.
  4. Onye gagbaso uzo
    Nk’onwu nime aro nka,
    Ka ndele na nkpa-n’aka-I
    Nenye ya nkasi-obi.
  5. K’ayi di ocha, zue oké,
    K'ayi buru nke-Gi mgbe dum;
    Yer'ayi aka k’ayi kwesi
    Inata okpu-eze. Amen.
