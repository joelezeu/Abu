"Onye-nwe-ayi, zi ayi ikpe ekpere"
Luk 11. 12.
Lord, teach us how to pray aright
  1. Jisus, zi ayi ikp’ekpere
    N'egwu na nsopuru;
    M’ayi bu'ntu n’aja n’iru Gi,
    Ayi aghagh ibia nso.
  2. Mgb'ayi nekpegh ayi nala n’iyi:
    Nye ayi ike kpere!
    M’ayi jikere ibiakuite I,
    Jisus, zut'ayi n’uzo.
  3. Nye nwuta nso n’ihi nmehie,
    Nye obi um’ala,
    N’ochicho sir’ike inu
    Olu-Gi, we di ndu.
  4. Okwukwe n’Aja nke nani
    Ya pur'ikpuchi njo;
    K'ayi wer'any'ayi, n'olil any'ayi
    Le nani Kraisi ingbe dum.
  5. K’ayi were ntacl'obi neche;
    M’ebere-Gi n’odu;
    Nye obi nada, mb'ayi ike
    Itukwasi I obi.
  6. Nye ndia, k'ewe me uche-Gi;
    Site n'ike-Gi du n,
    K'ayi si n'Okpara na Mo Nso
    Kpere Gi k'I si cho! Amen.
