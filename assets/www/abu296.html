UMU-NTAKIRI.

"Biko, O'nye-nwe-ayi' . . . me kwa ka ihe nagara oru-Gi nke-oma ta."
Nehemaia 1. 11.
The morning bright with rosy light.
  1. Ihé oma
    Nke ututu
    Akpotewom n'ura;
    Nna, nani Gi,
    N'ihu-n’anya,
    Nedebe nwa-nta-Gi.
  2. Ekperem Gi
    N'ubochi ta
    K'I burum Onye-nche;
    Gbaghara njom,
    Kwe ka m’nebi,
    Jisus, n'akuku-Gi.
  3. Nezu ike
    Nime obim,
    Mo Nso nke amara;
    Ka m'di ka Gi,
    M’we jikere
    Ihu iru-nso-Gi. Amen.
