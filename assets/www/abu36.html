ONWU JISUS

"O gwuwo."
Jon 19. 30.
Hark! the voice of love and mercy.
  1. Ge nti! olu ihun'anya
    Nke nada na Kalvari;
    Le, o nagbawa kwa nkume,
    Kwaghar'uwa, kpuch'igwé;
    "O gwusiwo,"
    Nur’ol’Onye-nzoputa.
  2. "O gwusiwo" Le, ob'uto
    Okw'ebube ndia nyere!
    Ngozi igwe Kraist nerudata.
    "O gwusiwo,"
    Ka ndi-Ya chet'okwu nka.
  3. "O gwusiwo." onyinye dum
    Bu nk'iwu mgb'ochie nwere;
    Emezuwo nkwa Chineke;
    Onw'apugh iy'egw'ozo.
    "O qwusiwo."
    Nsi-Ya nwe nkasi-obi.
  4. Ndi-m'ozi, chir'ubo-unu,
    Tob'aba Immanuel.
    Ndi bi n'uwa, ndi bi igwe,
    Soronu n'abu nmeri.
    Haleluya!
    To Nw'aturu Chineke. Amen.
