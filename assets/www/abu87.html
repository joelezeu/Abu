NZUKO KRAIST N'ELU-UWA.

"O gabu kwa eze rue mgbe nile ebigh-ebi"
Nkpuche 11. 15.
Zion's King shall reign victorious.
  1. Eze Zaion gachi na nmeri,
    Uwa dum gekwere Ya;
    Al’eze-Ya di otuto,
    O gachi mgb’ebigh-ebi.
  2. Mba ezonariri Chuku
    Gahu ihé otuto;
    Abali gagho ehihie,
    Igwé nuria n’ihu ya.
  3. Nd’Israel gbasara ger’uju
    Cho Chuku, Onye-nwe-ha,
    Le Y’anya, Ony’amapuru,
    Tikue Ony'akpogburu.
  4. Ez’uku, setipu aka-I,
    Kwagide ol’otuto-I;
    Wetara mba dum iye-aka,
    Zo ha, ha gefebe Gi! Amen.
