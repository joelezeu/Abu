UMU-NTAKIRI.

"Aka-Gi gedurum, Aka-nri-Gi gejidekwam"
Abu Oma 139. 10.
God is always near me.
  1. Chineke nanom nso,
    Nanu nke m’nekwu;
    O masiri echichem,
    N'olum, na egwum.
  2. Chineke nanom nso
    Nime abali;
    O nahu n’ochichiri
    Dika n’ehihie.
  3. Abum nwatakiri,
    M'O nom nso mgbe dum;
    Ile’anyam, n’okwum, n’obim,
    O mazuru ha. Amen.
