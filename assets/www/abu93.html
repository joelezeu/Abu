UBOCHI ONYE-NWE-AYI

"Nka bu ubochi Jehova meworo; ayi geteghari egwu onu nuri kwa onu nime Ya"
Abu Oma 118. 24.
This is the day the Lord hath made.
  1. Nka b'ubohi Chuku doro,
    O kpo y'oge nke-Ya;
    K'igwé n’ala nuria n'otu,
    Neto Ya n’och'eze.
  2. Ta k'O si n’onwu bilite,
    Ik’Ekweusu ada;
    Ta ka ndi nso to nmeri-Ya,
    Nekwusa ike-Ya.
  3. Hosanna diri Eze-ayi,
    Okpara nso Devid!
    Yer’ayi aka, si n’oche-Gi
    Wetar’ayi nzoputa.
  4. Ngozi diri Ony’ahu ji
    Ozi nk’amara bia;
    Nke nabia n’aha Chineke
    K'O zoputa ndi-njo.
  5. Hosanna! otuto kasi
    Nzuko nk’uwa genye;
    Elu-igwe, eb'O nachi,
    Geto Ya kari nka. Amen.
