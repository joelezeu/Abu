ITO N'AMARA

"Obu nani otu ngozi ka i nwere, nnam! goziem, obuna mu onwem, nnam."
Jenesis 27. 38.
Lord, I hear of showers of blessing.
  1. Anuwom bayere ngozi
    Nk'I nenye ora n’efu;
    Miri-ngozi netute ndu-
    Biko, kwe k’o zokwasim,
    Zokwasim!
  2. Agabigalam, Nn’amara!
    N’ezie nmehie di n’obim;
    I pur’irapum, ma kama
    K’ebere-gi biakwasim,
    Biakwasim!
  3. Onye-nwem, agabigalam!
    Ka m’jide, ma Hu I n’anya;
    Anamacho amara-Gi,
    N’obibia-Gi kpom oku,
    Kpom oku!
  4. Agabigalam, Mo d’ike!
    Gi nagwo ndi kpur’isi;
    Ony’ama olu nke Jisus,
    Wer’ike-Gi gwam okwu,
    Gwam okwu!
  5. Ihu-n'anya nenwegh ngbanwe!
    Obara di ok’onu!
    Amara di n’ebe nile!
    Nimem, biko, bulie ha,
    Bulie ha! Amen.
