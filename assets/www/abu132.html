INYE-IKE.

"Jehova mem otu a, mekwamihe ozo otu a, mgbe ihe nabugh onwu onwe-ya gekewa mu na gi"
Rut 1. 17.
O perfect Love, all humans thought transcending.
  1. Gi Ihu-n’anya, kachas'uche madu,
    N’ekpere k’ayi nada n'och’eze-Gi,
    Ka ha nwe ihu-n’anya nadigide,
    Bu ndi I jikoro ebigh-ebi.
  2. Gi Ndu zur’ezu, nye ha nghota nke-Gi,
    Nk'obi~oma n’okwukwe sir’ike,
    Nk’olil’anya, n’ezi nkasi-obi,
    Na ntukwasi-obi nenwegh egwu.
  3. Nye ha ohu neme k’ahuhu di nfe,
    N ’udo neme k’og’uwa nka dajua;
    Nye ubochi ndu-ha echi mara nma,
    Mgbe chi gabo n’ihu-n’anya na ndu.   Amen
