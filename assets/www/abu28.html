OMUMU JISUS

"Ha gakpo kwa aha-Ya Immamuel."
Matiu 1. 23.
Joy fill our inmost heart to-day.
  1. Onu juru obi-ayi ta;
    Amuru Nwa Eze:
    Usu ndi-m’ozi nanuri
    N’ih'obibia-Ya ta.

    2. Koros:- Nuria onu! Nwa Chineke
            Biara. ibi n’uwa;
            O digh aha tokari’ nka:-
            Immanuel.

  2. Ayi negbu ikpere nye Ya
    Neto, nasopuru,
    Mgbe ayi huru Gi n’aka nne-I,
    Gi Nwa nke Chineke.
  3. Gi Ihe anekegh eke,
    Mukwas’ayi, Nwa di nso
    K'ayi deb,ubochi-Qmumu-I,
    Neté I n'obi-ocha.   Amen.
