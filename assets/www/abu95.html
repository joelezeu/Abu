UBOCHI ONYE-NWE-AYI

"Le ka ebe-obibi-Gi nile si buru ebe ahuru n'anya nke-uku, Jehova, nke usu nile nke neli."
Abu Oma 84. 1.
Pleasant are Thy courts above.
  1. Ulo-I n’igwé mara nma,
    N’al’ihé n’ihu-n’anya;
    Ulo-I n’uwa mara nma,
    N'ala njo n'ahuhu nka.
    Agu nagu nkpur’obim
    Inwe nmeko ndi nso-Gi,
    Ihu onwunwu iru-I,
    N’izu-oké amara-I.
  2. Nnunu nabu ab’onu
    N’ebe-ichu-aja-Gi;
    Obi onu nez’ike
    N'obi Nna nk’elu-igwe!
    Dika nduru fur’uzo
    Nahugh ebe-iz’ike,
    Ha gala n'ugbo nke-ha,
    Nodu n’onu mgbe nile.
  3. Ndi no n'onu neto Gi
    Obuna nim'uwa nka;
    Miri nasu n'ozara,
    Nri si n’igwé nazu ha,
    Ha naga n’i1°u 1i’ike
    We rue n’oche-eze-Gi;
    Nada n’ukwu-Gi neto
    Onye duru ha n’udo.
  4. Nna, ka m’gbat'ih'ozo nka!
    Durum ga n’aj’uwa nka;
    Wor’amara-Gi debem,
    Nyem onodu n'ulo-Gi.
    Anyanwu n’Ota k’I bu,
    Dur-obi-nfughari nkem;
    Gi nw’otuto n’amara,
    Ka ha dirim, Onye-nwem! Amen.
