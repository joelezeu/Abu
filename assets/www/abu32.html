ONWU JISUS

"Ha we dupu Ya ka ha kpogide Ya n'obe"
Mak 15. 20.
There is a green hill far away.

  1. Ugwu di n’ebe di anya,
    N’azu ot'obodo,
    Ebe akpogburu Jisus,
    Nke nwuru n'ihi ayi.
  2. Ayi apugh iko akuko
    Ihe-nwuta nke-Ya;
    Ayi kwere n’o bu n’ihi ayi
    K’O huru ahuhu.
  3. O nwuru k'agbaghara ayi,
    K’ayi we gho ndi oma,
    K’ayi la n’igwé n’ikpe-azu,
    N’ike obara-Ya.
  4. O digh onye ozo puru
    Ikwu ugwo nke njo;
    Nani Ya pur"imegh'uzo
    Nke igwé, k’ayi bata;
  5. O huru ayi n’anya nk’uku;
    Ayi gahu-kwa-ra Ya,
    Tukwas’obi obara-Ya,
    Nalu olu nke-Ya.   Amen.
