OMUMU JISUS

"Ka ayi je rue Betlehem ugbu a,"
Luk 2. 15.
Oh come, all yé faithful, joyful and triumphant.

  1. Bianu, nd'okwukwe,
    N'onu nke di uku,
    Bianu, bianu, k’ayi je rue Bethlehem;
    L’Eze ndi-m'ozi,
    Nke amuru n’uwa;
    Bianu' k’ayi sopuru Ya,
    Bianu, k’ayi sopuru Ya,
    Bianu, kfayi sopuru Ya, bu Eze Kraist.
  2. Chuku, Nwa Chuku,
    Ihé ebigh-ebi,
    Le, O leligh afo nke Nw'agbogho;
    Ezi Chineke,
    Nke anekégh éké;
    Bianu, k’ayi sopuru Ya,
    Bianu, k’ayi sopuru Ya,
    Bianu, k’ay1 sqpuru Ya, bu Eze Kraist.
  3. Nd'abd ndi-m’ozi,
    Buanu n’oke Qnu,
    Buanu, unu ndi nile bi n'igwé;
    Otuto diri
    Chineke n’el’igwe;
    Bianu, k’ayi sopuru Ya,
    Bianu, k’ayi sopuru Ya,
    Bianu, k'ayi sopuru Ya, bu Eze Kraist.
  4. Ayi Skele Gi,
    Onye amuru ta;
    Jisus, otuto nile diri Gi;
    Okwu Chineke,
    Onye ghoro madu;
    Bianu, k’ayi sopuru Ya,
    Biahu, k’ayi sqpuru Ya,
    Biarm, k'ayi Sqpurp Ya, bu Eze Kraist.   Amen.
