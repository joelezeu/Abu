ATO NIM'OTU

"N'ebe nhu ka Jehova nyere ngozi ahu iwu"
"Abu Qma 133. 3."
Command Thy blessing from above.

  1. Chineke Nna zite ngozi
    Nye ayi, ndi zukQrQ n’ebe a;
    Le ayi, anya n’ihu-n’anya,
    Bu umu nasopuru Gi.

  2. Kraist Qkpara zite. ngozi,
    Kwe k’ayi so uzo-Gi n'ezie;
    Me k’Okwu-Gi -ba n'obi-ayi,
    Nye ayi ike nke ndu ohu.

  3. Mo nke di nso zite ngozi,
    Wer’ike-Gi mej’u1o nka;
    Mukwasi ndi di um'a1a,
    Kpughere ha amara-Gi.

  4. Chineke, Ato nim’Otu
    Nna na Qkpara na Mo Nso,
    Eb'ayi nario I n’otu obi,
    K'ayi fegide Gi mgbe' nile.   Amen.
