BAPTISM.

"Neme ha baptism ba n’aha nke Nna., na nke Okpara, na nke Mo Nso."
Matiu 28. 19.
Come, Holy Ghost, descend from high.
  1. Mo Nso, si n'e1u ridata,
    Gi Onye nasach’obi-ayi;
    Ka akéré nke amara,
    Ji miri nka gb’ama ugbu a.
  2. Wusa ike-I ka Chineke
    Fesa obara nke Jisus
    Ka Nna, n’Okpara, na Mo Nso.
    Do umu ndia umu Chuku.   Amen.
