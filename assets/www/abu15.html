CHINEKE NNA.

"Aha ahu nke kariri aha nile o bula elu"
Ndi Filipai 2. 9.
How sweet the name of Jesus sounds.

  1. Gi bu Uzo-O bu na Gi
    K’ayi nagbanari njo;
    Onye o bu1a gacho Nna,
    Gacho Ya nime Gi.
  2. Gi b’Ezi-okwu-okwu-Gi
    Nenye amam-ihe;
    Gi nwer'ike inye uche,
    Me k’obi di ocha.
  3. Gi b'ezi Ndu-nbilite-Gi
    Nekwusa nmeri-Gi;
    Ndi tukwasiri Gi obi
    Gagbanari Qnwu.
  4. Gi b'Uzo, Ez'okwu, na Ndu;
    K'ayi mar'Uzo ahu,
    Jid'Ez'okwu, nwe Ndu ahu,
    N'onu ebigh-ebi.   Amen.
