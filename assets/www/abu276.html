"Onye-nwe-ayi, Gi onwe-gi matara ihe nile onwe-gi mara na I bu eyim "
Jon 21. 17.
Jesus, friend of little children.
  1. Jisus, Ey’umu-ntakiri,
    Buru Eyi nkem!
    Jid’akam, debem mgbe nile
    Na nso Gi.
  2. Zim otu m’geto n’idi-nma
    Ubochi nile;
    Gi buri nwata, na n’ezie
    I mawo!
  3. Nedum na nzo-ukwum nile
    M'naga ka nwata;
    Ka m’d’ike, nwe amam-ihe
    N'ezi'okwu-I.
  4. Arapulam, aju-kwa-lam,
    Bur’Eyim mgbe dum!
    N'ihi na nkpa-Gi nakpam na
    Ndum nile. Amen.
