ATO NIM'OTU

"N'ihi na ndi ahu ndi nagba ama, di Ato."
1 Jon 6. 8.
Father of heaven, whose love Profound.
  1. Nna nk’igwé, Nke hur’ayi n'anya,
    We cheputar’ayi ngbaputa,
    Ayi nd’ojo gburu I ikpere;
    Biko, gbaghar'ayi njo nile.
  2. Okpara, Okwu Chineke,
    Onye-ngbaputa, n’Eze-ayi,
    Ayi nd’ojo gburu I ikpere;
    Wer’amara-Gi zoput’ayi.
  3. Mo Nso nke neme nkpur’obi
    K’o puta na njo na onwu,
    Ayi nd’ojo gburu I ikpere;
    Nye ayi ike nke ndu ohu.
  4. Jehova . . . Nna, Okpara, Mo . . . .
    Chineke, Ato, nim’Otu,
    Ayi nd'ojo gburu I ikpere:
    Gbaghara nmehie, nye ayi idu. Amen.
