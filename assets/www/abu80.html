AKWUKWO NSO

"Nara-kwa nu ... mma-agha nke Mo Nso nke bu okwu Chineke."
Ndi Efesos 6. 17.
Lamp of our fect.
  1. Oriona diri nkwu-ayi,
    Negosi ayi uzo;
    Is’iyi nk’esi n'amara
    Nk’el’igwe nye nd’ije.
  2. Nri nke nazi nkpur’obi-ayi,
    Ezi nri si n’elu;
    Ihe-ndu, ebe ayi nagu
    Bayere el’igwé.
  3. Ogidi-oku n’abali,
    Igw’oji n’ehihie;
    Mgbe ifufe nefegid'ayi,
    Ebe-ngbaba d’ike.
  4. Okwu Chuku ebigh-ebi,
    Uche Qkpara-Ya;
    I nedu nzo;-ukwu n’uwa,
    Nezi uzo nk'igwe.
  5. Chuku, kwe k’ayi muta nk’oma
    Amam-ihe nke-ya;
    Natughari ozizi-ya
    N'okwukwe nke nwata. Amen.
