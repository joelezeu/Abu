ONWU JISUS

"Okwu nke obe . . . buru ayi, bu ndi anazoputa, ihi nke Chineke."
1 Ndi Korint 1. 18.
We sing the praise of Him who died.
  1. Ayi neto Onye nke nwuru
    N’el’obe n’ihi ndi-nmehie:
    Madu nakwa Jisus emo:
    N’ihi nka ayi aju uwa.
  2. Onwu-Ya n'obe nezi ayi
    K'ihi-n'anya Chineke ra:
    O buru njo-ayi n'osisi,
    O wedatar’ayi ebere.
  3. Onwu-Ya nebupu nmehie,
    O netiye ndu n’obi-ayi;
    O nenye olile-anya,
    O neme k’ilu di uto.
  4. O newepu ujo n’obi,
    Nagba ndi nadigh ik’ume;
    O nachupu egwu ili,
    Nye ihé n’ubochi onwu.
  5. Kraist, Onye j'ahuhu d'ilu
    Wetara ndi-njo amara,
    Ka ndi nil’agbaputara
    Neto Gi rue mgb'ebigh-ebi. Amen.
