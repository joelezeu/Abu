ONWU JISUS

"O digh onye o bula nwere ihu-n’anya, kari nka uku."
Jon 15. 13.
The love that Jesus had for me.
  1. Ihu-n’anya Jisus jirim,
    N'ihi ahuhu n’osisi,
    K'O buru Onye-ngbaputam,
    Madu apugh iko!
    2. Koros:- Ihu-n'anya di ebube.
    Ihu-n’anya di ebube!
    Ihu-n'anya Jisus jirim.
    Madu apugh iko!
  2. O bur’ibu-obi d’ilu,
    Le, okpu ogwu O kpuru,
    Ka m’we kwa ndu mgbe nile,
    Madu apugh iko!
  3. Udo m'nwere nim'Onye-nwem,
    Onye nario n’iru Chuku,
    Olu obara-Ya luru,
    Madu apugh iko!
  4. Onu O Wetera ndi-Ya,
    Iz’ike ayi nenwe na Ya,
    Na ezi olile-anya,
    Madu apugh iko! Amen.
