CHINEKE OKPARA

"Onye jeghariri nalu olu oma."
Olu Ndi Ozi 10. 33.
Jesus, who lived above the sky.
  1. Jisus, Onye biri n'igwé,
    Ridatara gho madu nwua;
    Nim°Akwukwo Nso k'ayi gahu
    Aha madu oma O bu.
  2. O nejeghari neme nma,
    Nemeghe anya ndi-isi;
    Ndi nari’oria na, ndi-ngwuro
    O mekwa aru-ha d’ike.
  3. Nke kari nka O gwara ha
    Ihe nke Chineke nacho;
    O bu kwa Onye-omiko,
    O nege nti olu nwata.
  4. Ma O nwuru onwu ojo,
    Akpogburu Ya n’osisi;
    Aka-Ya O ji neme nma
    Ka ha kpogidere n’obe.
  5. Ma Akwukwo Nso koro ayi
    Ihe kpatara onwu-Ya;
    O biara gho madu, ma nwua
    K’ewe gbaghara nmehie-ayi.
  6. Chuku gaghara ndi nario,
    Nachos'ike ipu na njo;
    K’ayi choba iru-Ya ngwa ngwa,
    We ket’oké n’ebere-Ya; Amen.
