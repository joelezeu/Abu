ARO OHU

"N'ihi na anyanwu na ota, ka Jehova, bu Chineke bu."
Abu Oma 84. ll.
The day, O Lord, is spent.
  1. Jisus, chi ejiwo;
    Noyer’ayi, zur’ike;
    Nkpur’obi-ayi nachos'ike
    Ilezi Gi Obia.
  2. Ayi akerugh n'ala
    Ala onu ahu
    Ebe ndi-m’ozi-I neguzo,
    Anwu y’adigh-ada.
  3. Anwu nke-ayi nada,
    Ubochi-ayi nagwu;
    Gi Anwu nk’ezi-omume,
    Mukwasi ayi mgbe dum. Amen
