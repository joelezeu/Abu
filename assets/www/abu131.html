INYE-IKE.

"Ya me ka unu zue oke n'ezi ihe nile o bula ime ihe O nacho neme nime ayi ihe nato ezi uto n'iru Ya, site n'aka Jisus Kraist."
Ndi Hibru 13. 21.
Just as I am, Thine own to be.
  1. Otu m’di ibu nke-I, Onye
    Hurum n’anya, Ey’umu-nta,
    Ka m’do onwem nso, ma nye Gi,
    O Jisus Kraist-Abiam.
  2. N’ututu onu nk’ubochim
    Ka m'nye Gi ndum, mezu nkwa nkem,
    Me ngwa, newezugagh ihe,
    Wer’obim nile bia.
  3. Kraist, si n'aka-I amapuru,
    Wukwasi ha onyinye-Gi;
    Ka ndi guzoro n'iru Gi
    Neme uche-I n’ihu-n'anya.
  4. Agamebi n’ihe mgbe dum,
    M’galuru ih'oma olu,
    Were ikem nile fe Gi,
    Ya mere ka m’ji bia.
  5. Otu m’di, nwata, di ike,
    Ibu ezi’onye k’ikem ra,
    N ’ihi ezi’omume na Gi,
    Onye-nwe-ndum-Abiam.
  6. N’ihi Gi ka m’luzue olum,
    We nata kwa okpu'nmeri
    Bia togbo ya n'ukwu nke-Gi,
    Onye-nzotam-Abiam.   Amen.
