"Ubochi Onye-nwe-ayi."
Nkpughe 1. 10.
This day, at Thy creating word.
  1. Ubochi ta, n'Okwu nke-Gi
    Ka ekere uwa n’ihé;
    Chineke, mukwasi ayi ta,
    Mejue obi-ayi n’ihé-Gi.
  2. Ta ka Jisus, Onye nwuru,
    Biliri n’ike nke nmeri;
    Jisus, me k’ayi si n’onwu njo
    Soro Gi bilie, di kwa ndu.
  3. Ubochi ta Mo Nso biara
    N'ire-oku nke wasara;
    Mo Nso, mejue obi-ayi ta
    N'amara-Gi ikp’ekpere.
  4. Uboch'amara, ndu, n’ihé!
    Nk’iz'ike si n’olu nk’uwa!
    Oge nso-Gi nk'ihu-n'anya,
    Chineke, ayi nyeghachiri I.
  5. Otuto nile diri Nna,
    N’Okpara amuru n’anya,
    Na Mo Nso Onye ayi neto
    Rue mgbe nile ebigh-ebi. Amen.
