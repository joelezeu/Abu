UTUTU

"Anyasi, na ututu, na etiti-ehihie ka m'gatughari uche"
Abu Oma 55 17.
God will take care of you.
  1. Bia n’ekper’ututu,
    Bia k’ayi gbue ikperé;
    Ekpere b’ike ony’ije
    Ji eso Chineke.
  2. N’ehihie, zur’ike
    N’okpuru Nkume-ayi;
    Ndo-Ya bu ihe di uto
    Mgbe anyanwu nacha.
  3. N’anyasi, mechi’uzo,
    Kpe nime ulo-gi;
    I gahu n’o b'ulo Chuku,
    On'uzo el’igwé. Amen.
