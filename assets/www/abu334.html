ARO OHU

"Jehova gozie gi,debe gi: Jehova. me iru-Ya ka o mukwasi gi."
Onu-Ogugu 6. 24-25.
God will take care of you.
  1. Atul'egwu ih'o bula;
    Chineke gechebe i;
    Nodu nim’ihu-n’anya-Ya;
    Chineke gechebe i.
    2. Koros:-Chineke gechebe i
    N’ubochi nile k’uzo ra
    Ya onwe-Ya no nso:
    Chineke gechebe i.
  2. Olu meme ka ike gwu:
    Chineke gechebe i;
    Ihe-ize-ndu di n’uzo:
    Chineke gechebe i.
  3. Nkpa-gi nile ka O gegbo:
    Chineke gechebe i;
    O kwesiri ntukwas’obi:
    Chineke gechebe i.
  4. Iguzoro nenwegh eyi?
    Chineke gechebe i;
    O geme k’obi-gi d’ike:
    Chineke gechebe i.
  5. Nime onwunwa o bula,
    Chineke gechebe i;
    Ony'ume-ngwu, kwere na Ya!
    Chineke gechebe i! Amen.
