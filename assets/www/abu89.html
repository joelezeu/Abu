NZUKO KRAIST N'ELU-UWA

"Nna. Ka edo aha-Gi nso. Ka ala-eze-Gi bia"
Luk 11. 2.
Father, let Thy kingdom come.
  1. Nna, k’ala-eze-Gi bia,
    K’O nabia n’ike di ndu;
    Kwue okwu ikp'azu-Gi,
    Webata oge nmeri.
  2. Dik'o biara na mgb’ochie
    Nim'al'obi nke madu,
    Mgbe ndi oma nwuru Gi-
    Chineke, k'o bia ozo!
  3. Wep'och'eze nk’Ekwensu
    Kpop’arusi n’obe-ha;
    Chiba ochichi ka nma,
    Kpube okpu-eze-Gi.
  4. Otut’aro gar'aga
    K’I zir’ayi ekpere nka;
    Anya nile neche nche
    Ihu ihé-Gi nenwu.
  5. Ka chi nke Chineke bo!
    Obi-ayi we nuri’onu;
    Obi’uto n'abu di nso
    Gekele obubo-ya.
  6. K’och’eze na mba nile
    Buru ndi Chineke nwe;
    Nim’obi nke madu dum,
    Nna, k'ala-eze-Gi bia! Amen.
