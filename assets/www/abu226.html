ISO KRAIST

"Nechenu nche nekpe-kwa-nu ekpere, ka unu we ghara iba n'onwunwu"
Matiu 26. 41.
Christian, seek not yet repose.
  1. Onye-Kraist, achol’ura,
    Nur’olu Onye-nzota-i;
    I no n’etiti nd’iro:
    "Che nche, kpe."
  2. Nd’isi na nd’ike na njo
    Nachiko ndi-agha-ha,
    Nechére mgb’i nogh na nche:
    “Che nche, kpe.”
  3. Ke ihe-agha-i nk’igwé,
    Neyi ya oge nile;
    Ekwensu nech anwu:
    "Che nche, kpe."
  4. Ge nti olu ndi-nmeri;
    Ha nele ayi n’ogu-ayi;
    Ha ji otu olu si,
    “Che nche, kpe.”
  5. Nke kasi, nur'olu Kraist;
    Onye i huru n’anya;
    Zobe okwu-Ya n’obi-i:
    “Che nche, kpe."
  6. Che, dika na nani ya
    Genye i nmeri n’ogu-gi;
    Kpe, k’ezidat’iye’aka;
    “Che nche, kpe." Amen.
