NDI-IJE

"Udo diri u_mu-nga-ayi, ya na, ihu-anya nke okwukwe yiko, site na. Chlneke, bu Nna, Onye-nwe-ayi Jisus Kraist."
Ndi Efesos 6. 23.
For the dear once parted from us.
  1. N’ihi ndi-ayi no n’eb’ozo
    Ayi gabu n’ekpere-ayi:
    Wer’ihu-n’anya-Gi di nro
    Nke nech’umu-I n’ebe dum,
    Nna Nke di nso,
    Chebe ha na mgbe nile.
  2. Nime onwumwa na nsogbu,
    N’ih'egwu ubochi dum,
    Wer’omiko ebigh-ebi
    Narior’obi kpafuru,
    Onye-nzot’ayi,
    Debe ha n’uzo di nkpa.
  3. N’oge ujo n’ahuhu-ha,
    Mgb’anwanwa okwukwe-ha,
    Wer’amara-I nke natute
    Obi nke Kraist nwururu,
    Mo nk'amara,
    Bur'Onye-ndu ha mgbe dum.
  4. Nim’onu n’etiti ndi’eyi,
    N’ogu megide nd’iro,
    Ka ngozi-Gi noyere ha,
    K’iru-Gi nye iz’ike,
    Chuku d'ike:
    Nna, n’Okpara, na Mo Nso. Amen.
