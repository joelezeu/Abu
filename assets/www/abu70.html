OMUMU JISUS

"O genye kwe, unu Onye-nkasi-obi ozo, ka O noyere unu rue mgbe ebigh-ebi"
Jon 14. 16.
O Breath of God, breathe on us now.
  1. I Mo Nso, kuye ayi ume-Gi,
    Dube ayi mgb’ayi, nekp'ekpere;
    Is’iyi nke ndu ohu-ayi,
    Ez’ihé nk’ubochi ohu
  2. Onye-nw'ayi, I noyere ayi,
    M'adigh amazu k’I si bia;
    Na uso k’z1yi ganu olu-Gi,
    Mo-Gi gabokwu n'obi-ayi
  3. Kraist b'onu-nekwur'ayi n'elu,
    Gi b’Onye mario nime ayi;
    Biko, kpeputa eziokwu,
    Zaghachi okwu nmehie dum
  4. A! Obim nenwegh okwukwe!
    Mu mar-uzo, mar'Onye-ndum;
    Gbagharam, Eyim, Chineke,
    N'otutu oge m'fur'efu
  5. Noyerem mgb'ey'ozo nadigh
    Gamata. ihe-ngbu nk'obim
    Mgb'ih’egwu di, k'amata Gi
    N'aha ka mma - Onye nkasi!   Amen.
