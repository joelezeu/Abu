ATO NIM'OTU

"Chinekem ka I bu, anamachosi Gi ike."
Abu Oma 63. 1.
Holy Father, hear me.
  1. Nna di nso, nur’olum;
    Gi bu Onye-nzotam;
    Nanom nso mgbe nile,
    Gi Onye-amara.
  2. Jisus, Onye-nwe-ayi,
    Eze indu n'ebube,
    Ka mgbe ahu bia ngwa,
    Mgbe m’gada n’iru, Gi
  3. Mo Nso, Onye-nkasi,
    Nke bi nime obim,
    Mazuru nkpam nile,
    Doruem nso I, mem nke-Gi.
  4. Nso, Nso, Nso,
    Bia, arapukwalam;
    N’obim nke di ala,
    K'I gebi mgbe nile. Amen.
