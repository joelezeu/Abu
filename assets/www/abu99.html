UBOCHI ONYE-NWE-AYI

"M'gebili jekuru nnam, m’gasi kwa ya, Nna, emehlewom megide elu-igwe, na n’iru gi."
Luk 15. 18
Father, again in Jesus name we meet.
  1. I Nna, ayi zukot'ozo n’aha Jisus,
    Kpo isi ala nye I na ncheghari;
    Ayi we1i’ozo olu nadigh nké;
    Nario ebere, nabu ututo-I.
  2. Ayi gagozi ncheb’ebigh-ebi-Gi,
    Nekwuputa olu-Gi kw’ubochi;
    Ejigh ebere-I nekpube ndu-ayi?
    O bugh aka-I gbar’ayi buruburu?
  3. Ewo! ayi ekwesigh ihu-n’anya-I;
    Otutu mgbe k’ukwu-ayi fur’efu!
    M’ugbu a, n’ih’o1u-Gi nagb’ayi ume,
    Ayi ndi-njo lata n’ulo-Gi ka Nna.
  4. N’ih’Aha nk’ozuzu bi nime Ya,
    Site n'ihu-n’anya nke kachasi,
    N’Obara nk’awusiri n’ihi njo,
    Meghe uzo ebere kpobat'ayi! Amen.
