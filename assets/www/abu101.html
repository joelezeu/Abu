UBOCHI ONYE-NWE-AYI

"Nadinu n'udo: Chineke nke ihu-n'anya na udo ganoye-kwa-ra unu"

Lord dismiss us with Thy blessing.
  1. Nna, were ngozi-I zila'ayi,
    Mejue ayi n'onu n’udo;
    K'ayi nile nwe ihu-n'anya-I,
    Nanuri na nzoputa-I.
    Nagb'ayi ume,
    Nd’ije n’Qhia uwa nka.
  2. N'ihi olu Ozi-oma
    Ayi nekele, neto Gi;
    K’amia nkpuru nzoputa-Gi
    Nim'obi-ayi na ndu-ayi.
    Ka iru-Gi
    Noyeré ayi mgbe nile.
  3. Mgb'agamu olu nke opi,
    Ayi gesi n’uwa bilie;
    Were nku m'ozi la n'igwe,
    Neji onu za oku,
    Mgb'ebigh-ebi
    K’ayi na Kraist buko Eze.   Amen.
