UBOCHI ONYE-NWE-AYI

"Ala-eze nke uwa aghqwo ala-eze nke Onye-nwe-ayi na nke Kraiet-Ya."
Nkpughe 11. 15.
Jesus shall reign where'er the sun.
  1. Jisus gachi n'ebe nile
    Anyanwu naga ije-ya;
    Al’eze-Ya ganugbasa
    Rue mgbe onwu nagagh-adi.
  2. Agekpere Ya ekpere,
    Kpube Ya okpu otuto;
    Aha-Ya di k'isi uto
    Nk’aja ekpere ututu.
  3. Ndi nasu asusu nile
    Gabu ihu-n'anya nke-Ya;
    Ol’umu-nta gabekwusa
    Ngozi-ha n'elu aha-Ya.
  4. Ngozi nadi n’al’eze-Ya;
    Ndi-nkporo nwere nmeghepu,
    Nd’ike gwuru nezu ike,
    Anagozi ndi nkpa nakpa.
  5. K’ora nile bilie weta
    Ihe isopur’Eze-ayi;
    Ndi-ozi ji abu rida,
    Ndi bi n’uwa zanu. Amen.
