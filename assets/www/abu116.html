INYE-IKE.

"Nemeru nka ka unu we nechetam"
Luk 22. 19.
According so Thy gracious word.
  1. Jisus, dik’Okwu-Gi si di,
    N’obi ume-ala,
    Nka ka m’geme, Onye nwuru,
    Agamecheta Gi.
  2. Aru-Gi anyawara nyem,
    Gabu nrim si n’igwé;
    Agamahu n’iko ngozi-I,
    Were ya cheta Gi.
  3. M’ganechefu Getsemane?
    M’gahu igba-ngba-Gi,
    N’osus’obara n’ahuhu-I,
    Ghara icheta Gi?
  4. Mgbe m’chere iru n’obe-Gi,
    Lekwasi Kalvari:
    Nw’aturu Chuku, Aja nkem,
    Aghaghm-icheta Gi;
  5. Cheta Gi, n'ihe-ngbu-Gi dum,
    N'ihu-n'anya-I n'arum;
    Ebe ume-ndu foduru,
    Agamecheta Gi.
  6. Ma mgbe Onum kpuchitara,
    M'akpa-uchem lara,
    Mgbe I gabia n'al'eze-Gi,
    jisus, biko, chetam. Amen.
