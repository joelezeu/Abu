OMUMU JISUS

"Onu-uzo-ama, bulienu isi-unu elu;... Eze ebube gabata kwa."
Abu Oma 24. 9.
The golden gates are lifted up.
  1. Anebuli on'uz'ama,
    On'uzo gher'oghe;
    Ez'ebube agabawo
    Inoyere Nna-Ya.
  2. Kraist, I bur’ayi uzo rigo
    ldozir’ayi ebe,
    K'ayi we no n'eb'I no ugbu a,
    Hu kwa iru Chuku.
  3. Ih'otuto nenwu mgbe dum
    N'uzo-ayi nim'uwa;
    Ihe din'azu igw'oji
    Nke zoro Gi n'any'ayi.
  4. Bulite obi-ayi elu,
    Nye ayi amara-Gi;
    Ayi nawaghari n'uwa nka,
    M'abu-ayi di n'igwe.
  5. Ka eb'I no, n'aka-nri Nna,
    Olil'any'ayi gadi;
    Nogide nime ayi, k'ayi we
    Nogide kwa nime I.   Amen.
