CHINEKE OKPARA

"Jehova bu ezi omume-ayi."
Jer. 23. 6.
Jesus, Thy blood and righteousness.
  1. Jisus, uwem di ebube
    B'ez'omume n’obara-Gi;
    Mgbe uwa nile gagb’oku,
    Agamanuri kwa onu
  2. Ndu na onwu enwegh ike
    Imebi uw'ebube nkem;
    Uwe oma Jisus nenye
    Nadi ohu rue mgbe nile
  3. Mgbe m'si n’onwu, bilie ije
    N'ulo omam di n’el’igwe;
    Mgb’ahu, ngopum nile gabu:
    Jisus di ndu, nwu kwa n’ihim.
  4. N'uboch’ahu m’gagh-at’ujo,
    Onye pur'ibom ebubo?
    Obara Jisus newepu
    Nkpocha, n'ihere nke nmchiem.
  5. Ka ndi nwur’anwu nur’olu-I;
    Jisus, gwa ndi nke-Gi nuria;
    Obara-I n'ezi-omume-I
    Bu uwe ebube nke ha.
  6. Chuku nk'ihu-n’anya n’ike,
    Kwe k’uwa mara ebere-I
    Ka okwu nke-Gi gbasa kwa.
    Meri onwu na oku~mo. Amen.
