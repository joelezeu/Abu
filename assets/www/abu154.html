OTUTO

"Nyenu Jehova nsopuru aha-Ya."
Abu Oma 29. 2.
To God be the glory, great things He hath done.
  1. Otuto diri Chuku n'ih'O luru!
    O hur’uwa n’ar1ya, nye Okpara-Ya,
    Nke togboro ndu-Ya ikpuchi nmenie,
    Mehepu uzo ndu k’ora we bata.
    2. Koros:-To Chuku! To Chuku! K’uwa
          nur’olu-Ya!
  2. To Chuku! To Chuku! Ka madu
    dum nuria!
    Biko, biakute Nna site na Jisus;
    Nye ya otuto n’ok'ihe O luru!
  3. Ezi ngbaputa nk'eji obara go!
    Onye kwere ka nkwa Chineke diri;
    Onye kachasi njo nke kwere n'ezie,
    Jisus gagbaghara nmehie-ya ugbu a.
  4. Ok'ihe O zir’ayi, ok'ili'O luru,
    Ok’ohu k’ayi nwere site na Jisus;
    M'ayi ganahuri onu nke kachasi,
    Neti nkpu n’ebube mgbé ayi gahu Ya. Amen.
