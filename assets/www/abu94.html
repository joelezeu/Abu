UBOCHI ONYE NWE-AYI

"Debe ubochi-izu-ike, ido ya nso"
Deut. 5. 12.
This is the day of light.
  1. Ta b’ubochi ihé,
    Me ka ihé di ta;
    Jisus, bia n'ochichiri-ayi,
    Chupu ngbachi-ya dum.
  2. Ta b'uboch'iz'ike,
    Nye ayi ike onu;
    Nim'uche n’ob’ike gwuru
    Tuye ike ndu-Gi.
  3. Ta b’ubochi-udo,
    Mejue ayi n'udo-Gi;
    Wepu adigh-n'otu nile,
    Chupu ise-okwu.
  4. Ta b’uboch’ekpere,
    K’uwa n’igwé bia nso;
    Welie obi-ayi icho Gi,
    Bia zute ayi n’ebe a.
  5. Ta b’ubochi nke-nbu;
    Zipu ume ndu-Gi,
    Kpote ndi-njo ka ha to Gi,
    Onye-nmeri onwu. Amen.
