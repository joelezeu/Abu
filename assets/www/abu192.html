OKWUKWE

"Onye-nwe-ayi Jisus Kraist, Onye nwaru n'ihi ayi ka o gabu ka ayi we so Ya diko ndu."
1 Ndi Tesalonia 5. 9-10.
And can it be that I should gain.
  1. O gabu na m’gerit’uru
    N’obara Onye-nzoputam?
    O nwururum; onye mere
    K'O nwe ihe-ngbu, ga n’onwu!
    Ihu n’anya! o gadi’aha
    Na Gi, Chinekem, ganwurum?
  2. Ih'omimi! Okpar'anwua!
    Onye gamata nzube-Ya?
    N’efu ka ndi-m'ozi nanwa
    Iiu omim’ihu-n’anya.
    Ebere! K‘uwa sopuru!
    Ndi-m’ozi, ajula ozo!
  3. O rapuru och’eze Nna-
    Amara-Ya nenwegh nsotu-
    O mer'onwe-Ya ih’efu,
    Gba obara n’ihi ndi-njo.
    Ebere! enyere n’efu;
    Chinekem, o choputaram!
  4. N'oge mbu mom eker’agbu
    Togboro na njo n’ochichi;
    Ihé-I nwubara, m’we teta;
    Ola-nkporo juru n’ihe;
    Nkporom dapuru, m'nwer'onwem,
    Ebilirim, pua, soro Gi.
  5. Atugham egwu ikp-omuma;
    Jisus, n'ihe-Ya dum, bu nkem!
    Adim du mime Ya: Isim;
    Ezi'omume di nso b’uwem.
    N'ob’ike m'biarue ns'och'eze,
    Nat'okpu-eze n’aka Kraist. Amen.
