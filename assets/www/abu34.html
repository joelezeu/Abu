ONWU JISUS

"M'getiye gi nime oghere nke oke nkume ahu"
Opupu 33. 22.
Rock of Ages, cleft for me.
  1. Nkume mgbe ebigh-ebi,
    Kwe ka m’zoro nime Gi;
    Ka miri na obara
    Nke si n’akuku-I puta,
    Buru ih'isacha njom,
    Nzoputam n’ike nke-ya.
  2. O bugh olu nke akam
    Gemezu iwu nke-Gi;
    M’jisiwod’ike mgbe dum,
    Nakwa kw’akwa mgbe nile,
    H’apugh iwepu nmehiem:
    Nami Gi gazoputam.
  3. Agbam aka biakute I,
    Amakurum obe-Gi;
    Agbam oto nim’obim,
    Nyem uwe, yerem aka;
    Abiam n’is’iyi n’inyi;
    Onye-nzoputam, sacham.
  4. Ebe m’nek’ume udu nka,
    Mgbe m’mechir’anyam n'onwu,
    Mgbe m’gala n’uwa ozo,
    Hu Gi n’oche-ikpe-Gi,
    Nkume uke ebigh-ebi,
    Kwe ka m’zoro mime Gi. Amen.
