ORIRI NSO.

"Nka bu arum, nke aneny, n'ihi unu bu ogbugba-ndu ohu nime obaram."
1 Ndi Korint 11. 24-35.
Twas on that night when doomed to know
  1. N'abai'ahu k’arara Ya
    Nye n’aka iwe nd’iro dum,
    Ka Onye-nzoputa uwa
    Ji nara ogbe achicha.
  2. Mgb’O nyesiri Ya ekele,
    Bu Onye nwe uwa n’igwé.
    Q nyaw’ih’atu nk’aru-Ya,
    Nye ndi nes’uzo-Ya, nasi:-
  3. "Arum nke anyawara nye
    N’ih’unu, nara, rie, di ndu:
    Mgbe nile unu geme ya,
    Necheta ihu-n’anya nkem."
  4. O nara iko n’aka-Ya,
    Nye Chuku otuto ozo;
    K’omiko nenwu n’obi-Ya,
    Nzoputa si n’onu-Ya bia.
  5. "Obara nkem k’awusiri
    K’o sacha obi nke nmehie;
    Ogbugba-ndu nka gosiri
    Amar'ebigh-ebi nk'igwe.
  6. Ihu-n’anya jur'iko nka,
    K'ora nua manya nso ahu;
    Nanunu ya n'ogbo nile,
    Necheta oge onwu nkem."   Amen.
