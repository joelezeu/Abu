ONWU JISUS

"Mgbe ha huru kpakpando ahu ha we duria oke onu nke-uku."
Metiu 2. 10.
As with gladness men of old.
  1. Dika nd’ochie ji onu
    Hu kpakpando dura ha;
    Wer'onu so ihé-ya,
    Nk'o ji nedu ha n’uzo;
    Kwe, Nn'ayi, ka esi otu a
    Duru ayi biakute Gi.
  2. Otu ha ji onu ga
    We rue n’ebe Jisus no;
    Gbue ikpere n'iru Ya,
    Ony’igwe n'uwa neto;
    Otu a me ka ukwu-ayi
    Ga rue och’ebere-Gi.
  3. Otu ha nyer'onyinye
    Nke di ok'onu-ahia;
    Otu a k’ayi were onu,
    Nke njo n’aghugho nadigh
    Weta aku-ayi nile,
    Nye Gi, Kraist, Eze nke-ayi.
  4. Jisus di nso, kw’ubochi
    Deb’ayi n’uzo nkpagide;
    Mgbe ihe uwa gafe,
    Kpobat’obi-ayi n’ebe
    Kpakpando agagh-edu,
    Eb'ebube-Gi nenwu.
  5. N’obodo oma nk’igwé
    Ha achogh ihe nk'uwa;
    Gi b’lhe na Qnu-ya,
    Gi Anwu namu mgbe dum;
    N'eb'ahu ayi ganabu,
    Neto Ez’ayi mgbe nile. Amen.
