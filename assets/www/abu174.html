"N'ihi na mgbe ayi no nadigh ike, mgbe oge-Ya ma Kraist nwueu n'ihi ndi nadigh-asopuru Chineke"
Ndi Rom, 5. 6.
Jesus, my Lord, to Thee I Cry.
  1. Onye-nwem, Gi ka m’kpokuru,
    Aghaghm inwu m’I yeghm aka;
    Werute nzoputa-Gi nso,
    Werem otu m’si di!
    2. Koros:-Werem otu m’si di,
    Werem otu m’si di!
    Jisus nwurum! bu aririom;
    Werem otu rn si dil!
  2. Ike adigh, ikpe maram,
    Ma I wusirim obara-I,
    I pur’imem ka I si cho;
    Werem otu m’si di!
  3. Apughm ijikere onwem,
    Ezi nzubem ka m'mebiri,
    Ma zoputam n’ihi aha-I;
    Werem otu m’si di!
  4. Onye-nzotam, lem n’ukwu-Gi,
    Mesom ka o si di Gi uma,
    Malite olu-I, lusia ya;
    Werem otu m’si di! Amen.
