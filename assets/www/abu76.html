AKWUKWO NSO

"N'ihi na ebe madu abua ma-obu ato zukoworo n'aham, n'ebe ahu ka M'no n'etiti ha"
Matiu 18. 20.
Thy presence. gracious Lord, afford.
  1. Bia n’etiti ayi, Chineke;
    Jiker’ayi inu okwu-Gi,
    Do nti-ayi n’inu olu-Gi,
    Nye ayi okwukwe k’ayi we nu.
    2. Koros:-Gozie ndi nejere I ozi,
            Me k’Ozi Oma-Gi gbasa.
  2. Wepuru ayi uche abua,
    Tukwasi obi-ayi n’elu;
    Nye ayi nri sitere n’igwé,
    Me k’afo nri nke ndu ju ayi.
  3. Wer’ike nke Mo Nso nke-Gi,
    Kua okwu nke-Gi n’obi-ayi,
    K’ayi wer'okwukwe na egwu
    Neme otu ayi nur’ugbu
  4. Nna, gosi ayi Okpara-Gi,
    Me k’ayi mar’ihe I nacho;
    Weputa ike-Gi n’ihé,
    Dusi ayi n’ala otuto. Amen.
