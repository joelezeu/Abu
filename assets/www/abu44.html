NBILITE-N'ONWU JISUS

"Bukuenu Jehova abu ohu; n’ihi na O luwo olu di iche iche."
Abu Oma 98. 1.
The strife is o’er, the battle done.
  1. Haleluya! Haleluya! Haleluya!
    Okwu agwu; ogu ebie:
    Jisus emeriwo onwu;
    Bubanu abu Ok’onu:
    Haleluya!
  2. Onwu mere k’ike-ya ra,
    Ma Kraist lugburu ya n'ogu;
    K’ayi tisie nkpu onu ike:
    Haleluya!
  3. Ubochi ato gabigara,
    O si n’onwu bilie, di ndu;
    Otuto nile diri Kraist!
    Haleluya!
  4. O kujiri nkporo onwu;
    O kwaghe uzo el'igwe;
    K'ayi buara Ya abu nmeri:
    Haleluya!
  5. Jisus, site n’onwu nke-Gi,
    Zoputa ayi n'egwu onwu,
    K’ayi di ndu, nabuku I abu
    Haleluya! Amen.
