OMUMU JISUS

"Mem ka m'zo ijo n'ezi-okwu-Gi zikwam ihe"
Abu Oma 25. 5.
Holy Spirit faithful Guide!
  1. Mo Nso, ezi Onye-ndu!
    Nano nso onye nke Kraist;
    Wer’aka-I dur’a nwayo,
    Nd’ike gwuru nahuri,
    Nanu ol’utQ nke~Gi
    Si, “Onye fur’uzo, bia,
    Sorom, agamedula il"
  2. Eyi kasi nma no nso
    Iyer’ayi aka mgbe dum;
    Arapukwal’ayi n’egwu,
    Naso isi n'ochichi.
    Mgbe ok'ifufe nefe,
    Ike n’olil’any’agwu,
    Si, “Onye fur'uzo, bia,
    Sorum, agamedula i!”
  3. Mgb’uboch'olu-ayi gagwu,
    Mgbe ayi neche iz’ike,
    Mgbe ekpere na igwé,
    Nani ha foduru ayi;
    Mgb’ayi gaf’osimir’onwu,
    Natukwasi Kraist obi,
    Si, “Onye fur'uzo, bia,
    Sorum, agamedula i!"   Amen.
