UBOCHI ONYE-NWE-AYI

"O kusa ha ume, si ha. Naramu Mo Nso."
Jon 20. 28.
Breathe on we, Breath of God.
  1. Kuyem ume, Chuku:
    Mejuem na ndu ohu,
    Ka m'we hu ihe-Gi n’anya,
    Neme kwa uche-Gi.
  2. Kuyem ume, Chuku,
    Rue mgb’obim di ocha,
    M’we nacho ihe I nacho,
    Nogide nime ya.
  3. Kuyem ume, Chuku,
    Rue mgbe m’gabu nke-Gi
    Anu-arum nka we nenwu
    Ka ihé nk’el’igwe.
  4. Kuyem ume, Chuku,
    Ka m'ghar’inwu anwu,
    Ma soro I'di ndu zur'oké
    Nke mgb'ebigh-ebi-Gi. Amen.
