UBOCHI ONYE-NWE-AYI

"Mu onwem gakpobuta kwa unu, buru kwa unu Nna, unu onwe-unu gabu-kwa-ram umu-ndikom na umu-ndinyem."
2 Ndi Koriot 6. 17-18.
O God, our strength our hope, our work.
  1. Chuku, Ike n’Olil’any'ayi,
    Onye nkwa-I adigh-ada,
    Nar'umu ndia mgb'ebigh-ebi
    N’otu nd’I j’obara gba.
  2. Ayi ewer'Ah'ato-nso-Gi
    Ka h'akara na miri;
    Nario I k’I nye ha onodu
    N’etiti-umu nke-Gi.
  3. Ayi kara obe-I n’iru-ha,
    Ih’ama nk’ahuhu-Gi;
    Ka nkwa ha kwer'ugbu a buru
    Nkwuputa ndu-ha mile.
  4. Ekwela, Nna, ka ndu n’onwu
    Kewapu ha n’eb'I no;
    Ka ha kwesi ntukwas’obi,
    Buru nke-I mgb’ebigh-ebi.   Amen.
