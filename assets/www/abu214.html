ITO N'AMARA

"O neweghachi nkpuru-obim: Onedum n'uzo nile nke ezi omume n'ihi aha-Ya"
Abu Oma 23. 3.
Saviour, lead me lest I stray.
  1. Jisus, nedum n’uzo ndu,
    Ka m’we ghara ikpafu;
    Anom n’udo n’akuku-I,
    Ka m’biri n’ihu-n’anya-I.
    2. Koros:-Nedum, nedum,
    Jisus, ka m’ghar’ikpafu;
    N’ogologo ndum nile,
    Onye-nwem, nedum nwayo.
  2. Gi, Ebe-ngbagé nk’obim!
    Mgb’ifufe nefes’ike,
    Anom n’udo m’I no nso,
    Gi bu ntukwasi-obim.
  3. Nedum, k’ebili-miri
    Nke ndum we gabigasia;
    Agameru al’ihé
    Eb'anya-miri nadigh. Amen.
