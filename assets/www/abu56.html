OMUMU JISUS

"Le O mabia, Ya na igwe-oji; anya nile o bulu gahuzu kwa Ya"
Nkupughe 1. 7.
Le! He come with clouds descending.
  1. Le! O si n'igwé narida,
    Ony’egburu n’ihi ayi;
    Otutu ndi nso neso Ya
    Nabu abil nke nmeri;
    Haleluya!
    Chuku abia ib'Eze.
  2. Anya nile gahu kwa Ya
    N'ebube-Ya di uku;
    Ndi nke kpapuru Ya, re Ya,
    Kpogide Ya n’osisi,
    Geji akwa
    Hu ezi Mesaia-ha.
  3. Ihe-ama nke onwu-Ya
    Di kwa n’aru Qma-Ya;
    Nka nakpatara ndi nke-Ya
    Ohu di mgb’eigh-egbi;
    Anya gini
    K'ayi ji le apa-utu-Ya!
  4. Ngbaputa, nk'eler’anya-ya,
    Aputawo n'ebube;
    Ndi nsQ-Ya, ndi uwa juru,
    e Geje zute Ya n’elu;
    Haleluya!
    Ubochi Chinek’abia.
  5. Amen! k’Qra madu to Gi,
    N’elu oche-eze-Gi;
    Were ike na ebube,
    Nara ala-eze-Gi;
    Biko, bi ngwa,
    Chinek’ebigh-ebi, bia!   Amen.
