IJERE KRAIST OZI

"Ihe nile o bula unu neme kwa, n'okwu ma-obu n'olu, nemenu ha nile n'ala Onye-nwe-ayi Jisus"
Ndi Kolosi 3. I7.
Father, who on man dost shower.
  1. Nna, Nke si n'ulo-aku-Gi
    Nawukwasi ayi onyinye,
    Nye ayi ike k’ayi we were
    Onyinye-Gi n’uz’oma.
  2. K’oge iz’ike di ocha,
    K’ayi jide onw’ayi na mgb’onu;
    K’ayi nwe aku n’uzo di nso,
    Aru n’obi zur’oké.
  3. Wepu, na mba nka na mba dum,
    Ihe nakpata odida;
    Tinyua oku nke onwunwa;
    Chughachi nd’iro-I azu.
  4. Noyer’ayi, neny’ayi ike-Gi,
    K’ayi wer’ike nadigh-anwu
    Logide nd'iro nke madu,
    Guzos’ike nim'ogu.
  5. Ochi-agha-ayi mgbe nile,
    Nedu ayi k’ayi nalus’ike;
    Ka Nzuko-Gi zoput'uwa;
    Ny’ayi ike n’amam-ihe.
  6. Nna, Nke choro we choput’ayi;
    Okpara, Nke hur’ayi n’anya;
    Mo Nso, Nke bi nim’obi-ayi;
    Ato nime'otu, nuru. Amen.
