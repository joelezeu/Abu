AKWUKWO NSO

"Kwue; n’ihi na oru-Gi nanu."
1 Samuel 3. 10.
Speak, Lord, in the stillness.
  1. Kwue, mgb’oge dajuru,
    Mgbe m’no neche Gi;
    Me k’obim nana nti,
    Neche okwu-Gi.
  2. Kwue, O Nna di ngozi
    N'oge a dajuru;
    Ka m’hu iru-Gi, Nna,
    Mara ike-Gi.
  3. N'ih'okwu nk'I nekwu,
    "Ha bu ndu" n'ezie;
    Nri nk'igwé nenye ndu,
    Zuba mom ugbu a!
  4. Ihe dum m'rapuru,
    Enweghkwam onwem;
    Onu ka m'ji nye Gi
    Ihe nile nkem.
  5. Kwue, Nna, n’oru-I nanu!
    Agbala nkiti;
    Nkpur'obim neche Gi,
    Okwu nenye ndu!
  6. Mejuem n'amam-ihe
    Nk’uch’ebube-Gi;
    K’ih’uto I nacho
    Zur’oké nimem.
  7. Dika "ala oma"
    Nke nasu miri
    Nesi isi oma,
    Ka m’no n'ru Gi! Amen.
