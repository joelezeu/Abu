CHINKEKE MO NSO

"otu ahu Mo Nso neye-kwa-ra adigh-ike-ayi aka."
Ndi Rom 8. 26.
Come to our dark nature’s night.
  1. Were ihé-I di ngozi
    Bia nim’ochichiri-ayi,
    Mo Nso di ebigh-ebi,
    Chuk’onye-nkasi.
  2. Ayi di njo-Gi sacha ayi;
    Nim'oria-nye ayi, ike-I;
    Ayi fur-efu-Gi chot'ayi;
    Chuk’Onye-nkasi.
  3. Obi-ayi bu ogbeye;
    Si n'ihwé nye ayi onu
    Okwukwe n'ihu-n’auya,
    Chuk'Onye-nkasi.
  4. K'udo-I da k'igirigi,
    Weda aj'ochicho nk'ayi,
    Gosi ayi ihe nke Kraist,
    Chuk’Onye-nkasi.
  5. Obia di nso, di nwayo,
    K’obi-ayi bur’ulo-nso-I
    Ebe I gachi k’Eze,
    Chuk'Onye-nkasi.
  6. Narior'ayi nim'obi-ayi,
    Nas'udé nke ananugh,
    Nario nkpa-ayi anekwugh
    Chuk’Onye-nkasi.
  7. Nakpo "Abba, Nna" nim'ayi,
    Ih'ama onu nk’igwé
    Na nke ndu ebigh-ebi,
    Chuk’Onye-nkasi.
  8. Chor’ayi omimi Chuku,
    Buru ayi n’uzo ihé,
    Me ka k’ayi rigorue n’ulo-I,
    Chuk’Onye-nkasi. Amen.
