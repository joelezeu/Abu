ISO KRAIST

"Onye were ngozi nile o bula nke Mo Nso gorie ayi n'ele di n'elu-igwe nime Kraist."
Ndi Efesos 1. 3.
When upon life’s billows you are tempest tossed.
  1. Mgb’ebili-miri nke ndu namaghari i,
    Mgb’i neché n’ihe nile emebiwo;
    Guk’otutu ngozi-gi n’otu n’otu,
    Olu Chineke luru geju i anya!
    2. Koros:-Guko ngozi-gi n’otu n’otu,
    Guko, k’i hu olu Chineke!
    Guko ngozi-i, kpo ha ot’otu;
    Olu Chineke luru geju i anya!
  2. O di mgbe ibu-obi nanyigbu gi?
    Ob’enyere i k’i buru, o di aro?
    Guk’otutu ngozi-i, ich’uche gala,
    I ganabu abu ka ubochi ra!
  3. Mgbe i huru aku n’ala ndi ozo,
    Cheta n’ekwere i aku nk’igwé na nkwa;
    Guko ngozi-i; ego apugh izuta
    Ugw’olu-gi n’ulo-gi nke di n’igwé.
  4. Otu a n’etiti ogu nke uwa nka,
    Ike agwula gi, Chineke b’eze!
    Guk’otutu ngozi-i; ndi-m’ozi no nso,
    Ha geyere i aka rue mgb’ije gagwu. Amen.
