UBOCHI ONYE-NWE-AYI

"O geru kwa na, tutu ha akpo, Mu onwem gaza: mgbe ha onwe-ha no nekwu okwu, Mu onwem ganu."
Aisaia 65. 24.
Lords we come before Thee now.
  1. Chuku, ayi abia ugbu a,
    N’ukwu-I k'ayi gbur-ikpere;
    Akpapula aririo,
    Ayi garioro Gi n’efu?
  2. Gi k'ayi tukwasir'obi,
    Wer'ebere ridata;
    Meju’obi-ayi n’amara,
    Me k'onu-ayi neto Gi.
  3. N’uzo nke I doziri
    Ayi biara ichota Gi;
    Chuku, ezipula ayi,
    Ghara inye ayi ngozi.
  4. Site n’ozi-oma-Gi
    Zite okw’udo n’onu;
    Ka Mo-Gi ny’obi nile
    Nzoputa-Gi zur’oké.
  5. Kasie obi ner'uju,
    K'oge onu laghachi;
    Palie ndi-Gi dar'ada,
    Nye ha okwukwe d'ike.
  6. Kwe k’ayi nile choputa
    Gi, bu Onye-ebere:
    Gwo ndi-oria nile nk'ayi,
    Zilag'ayi n'onu nke-Gi.   Amen.
