OMUMU JISUS

"O nyewo ayi ufodu nime Mo nke Ya"
1 Jon 4. 13.
Come, Holy Spirit, heavenly Dove.
  1. Bia, Mo Nso, Nduru nke igwe
    N’ike ndu-Gi nile;
    Muye oku ihu-n’anya
    N’obi-ayi jur’oyi.
  2. Ayi nabu abu-ayi n’efu.
    Ike nbili adigh;
    Otuto-ayi naju oyi,
    Ekpere nanyi ayi.
  3. Chineké, ayi gadigide
    N’ume-ngwu di otu a?
    Ihu-n’any'ayi ajuk’oyi,
    Ma nke-I ekpok’oku!
  4. Bia, Mo Nso, Nduru nke igwe,
    N'ike ndu-Gi nile;
    Musa oku ihu-n’anya-I
    Nke gafunwu nke-ayl.   Amen.
