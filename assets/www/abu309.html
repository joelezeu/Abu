ARO OHU

"Obuna ochichiri adigh-aghachiri Gi; Ma abali nenye ihé dika ehihie."
Abu Oma 139. 12.
Saviour, breathe an evening blessing.
  1. Jisus, nye ngozi anyasi
    Tutu ayi ezu ike;
    Njo na nkpa k’ayi nekwuputa:
    Gi nazopu, Gi nagwo.
  2. Ma nbibi di n’akuku-ayi,
    Aku efeghari ayi,
    Ndi-m’ozi-Gi nechebe ayi;
    Ayi no n'udo m’I no nso.
  3. M’abali gba ochichiri,
    O gagh-ezo iru-Gi;
    Ik'adigh-agwu Gi, Onye
    Nechebe umu-Gi dum. Amen.
  4. Iguzoro nenwegh eyi?
    Chineke gechebe i;
    O geme k’obi-gi d’ike:
    Chineke gechebe i.
  5. Nime onwunwa o bula,
    Chineke gechebe i;
    Ony'ume-ngwu, kwere na Ya!
    Chineke gechebe i! Amen.
